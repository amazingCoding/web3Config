1. After opening MetaMask, you can click on the button in the upper right corner to display more options.

2. From there, you can create a new wallet or import an existing one.

3. Make sure to switch to the correct network for the payment you wish to make.

4. Finally, scan the QR code to complete the payment.

1. Choose MetaMask as the payment method.

2. Click 'Connect MetaMask'.

3. In the pop-up page, select one of your wallets and click 'Next' (it will automatically connect next time).

4. After connecting, the wallet balance will be checked, and a confirmation pop-up will appear.
