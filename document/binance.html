Other Wallet Payment Tutorial

1. Go to Exchange or Exchange wallet and click ‘Withdraw’ (We use Binance Wallet as example here)

2. Select the corresponding crypto to make payment.

3. Scan the payment QR code or enter the payment address.

4. Make sure you select the right chain for the payment.

5. Enter the payment amount and confirm withdrawal.
