Desktop

To make a payment using TronLink, scan the QR code to enter the payment page and proceed with the payment. For instructions on how to create a wallet and other related information, please refer to the official documentation for TronLink.

1. Choose TronLink as the payment method.

2. Click 'Connect TronLink'.

3. In the pop-up page, select one of your wallets and click 'Connect' (it will automatically connect next time).

4. Click 'Pay'.

5. Confirm the payment in the pop-up page.
